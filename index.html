Flexmix

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Flexmix app handles your information. The App is built and published by HEYIU117. By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

Summary

Flexmix does not collect, transmit, or share your personal data with us or anyone else. Your settings and workout history are stored on your device and — if you're signed in to iCloud — sync privately through your own iCloud account so they're available on your other Apple devices. We never receive or have access to this information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information from you. The App does not require an account, does not ask for your name, email, location, contacts, photos, or any other identifying information.

Information Stored on Your Device

To make the App work, Flexmix saves a small amount of preference data using Apple's standard storage systems (UserDefaults and, when enabled, iCloud Key-Value storage). This may include:

  • Your selected workout schedule
  • Your preferred number of exercises per session
  • Your current tier selection (Core or Boost)
  • Your workout progress and completion history

We do not have access to this information. It is stored on your device and, if iCloud is enabled, in your private iCloud account (see iCloud Sync below). If you delete the App, the on-device copy is removed with it.

iCloud Sync

If you are signed in to iCloud, Flexmix uses Apple's iCloud (Key-Value storage) to keep the preferences listed above in sync across the Apple devices that use your Apple ID. This information is stored in your private iCloud account and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We operate no servers of our own and cannot see or access any of it. iCloud sync is optional — you can turn it off at any time in iOS Settings by tapping your name, then iCloud, and you can remove the synced data from the same iCloud settings.

Network Usage

Other than Apple's built-in iCloud sync described above, the App does not connect to any servers. We operate no servers of our own, and the App sends no analytics, crash reports, advertising identifiers, or any other data over the internet.

Third-Party Services

Flexmix does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect any personal information, the App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in the relevant jurisdiction).

Your Rights

Because we do not collect or have access to your data, there is nothing on our side for you to access, correct, or delete. You can remove your information at any time by deleting the App from your device and clearing its data from your iCloud storage settings.

Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact
