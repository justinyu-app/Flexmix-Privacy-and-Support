Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my workout schedule?

Open the app, go to the Settings tab, and tap Schedule. You can choose which days of the week to train and which days are rest days.

How do I change the number of exercises per session?

In Settings, tap "Exercises per session" and pick any number from 2 to 6 — or up to 12 with Boost.

What's the difference between Core and Boost?

Core gives you the essential workout randomizer with a curated exercise library. Boost expands the exercise library and unlocks additional features. You can switch tiers from the Settings tab.

How do I view the details for an exercise?

Tap any exercise card on the home screen. The card flips over to show a description, technique tips, and key form cues.

I want to regenerate today's workout. How?

Use the randomize button on the home screen to swap your current selection for a new mix.

Do my workouts sync across my devices?

Yes. If you're signed in to iCloud, Flexmix keeps your schedule, settings, and workout history in sync across the Apple devices that use your Apple ID. It all goes through your own private iCloud account, so I never see any of it. You can turn it off any time in iOS Settings — tap your name, then iCloud.

I got a new iPhone. How do I get my workouts back?

Sign in to the same Apple ID with iCloud turned on, install Flexmix, and open it — your schedule, settings, and progress sync down from iCloud automatically. If they don't show up right away, make sure iCloud is enabled in iOS Settings and reopen the app.

Does Flexmix collect my data?

No. Flexmix doesn't collect, transmit, or share your personal data — I have no servers and never see your information. Your settings and workout history stay on your device and, if you're signed in to iCloud, sync privately through your own iCloud account. See the Privacy Policy for full details.

I found a bug. What should I include in my report?

Please email me with: a short description of what happened, the steps to reproduce it, your iPhone model, and your iOS version. Screenshots are very helpful and make it much faster to track down the issue.

I have a feature request.

I love hearing ideas. Email me at the address above with your suggestion — I keep a running list of feature requests and prioritize the ones that come up most often.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple directly, not by me. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, and request a refund for Flexmix.
